Zip-Based Technology* (ZBT)*

A revolutionary approach to system efficiency and security.

ZBT automatically compresses and encrypts inactive data and resources at the kernel level, reducing storage footprint and enhancing security.

Double Type Bit Technology* (DTBT)*

An innovative image and data processing methodology.

DTBT intelligently splits incoming data streams, processes them, and combines them to form an enhanced output, increasing effective data density and processing fidelity.
